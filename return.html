Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment provider.

Who pays for return shipping?

For defective or wrong items, we provide a prepaid shipping label. For other returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

Can I exchange an item instead of a refund?

Yes! You can exchange for a different size, color, or product. Just indicate this in your return request.
